System Overview

What is this system?

  • Analyzes monthly biometric attendance data
  • Detects anomalies: late arrival, early departure, short hours, missing punches
  • Employees justify anomalies online
  • Group Heads review and accept/decline
  • Admin finalizes leave deductions

Key Features

  • Role-based dashboards (DG, Head, Employee, Admin)
  • Office-wise data (HQ + Regional Offices)
  • Excel & PDF report export
  • Holiday calendar management
  • eHRMS leave reconciliation
  • Complete audit trail

How to Review Justifications

Step-by-Step

  1. Login with your GH username (e.g. gh.hrmgroup)
  2. Dashboard shows all employees with anomaly counts
  3. Click "Review Justifications" button
  4. For each employee's submitted justification:
    • Read the justification text
    • Check supporting documents (if uploaded)
    • Select: Accept, Decline, or Query
    • Add your remark (optional but recommended for Decline/Query)
  5. Click "Submit All Decisions" at the bottom
  6. Use "Accept All" / "Decline All" for bulk actions
Note: You can only review justifications that have been submitted by employees. Pending (grey) items mean the employee hasn't submitted yet.

How to Submit Justifications

Step-by-Step

  1. Login with your username (e.g. dk.rahul)
  2. Click "View & Justify" on your attendance session
  3. You see your attendance summary + day-wise anomaly table
  4. For each anomaly date:
    • Check the anomaly type (Late, Early, Short Hours, Missing Punch)
    • Type your justification in the text box
    • Example: "Was on official tour to Ministry. Tour order attached."
  5. Click "Submit Justifications"
  6. Success message with timestamp will appear
  7. If Head sends a Query, you'll see it with the Head's remark. Update and re-submit.
Important: Submit justifications promptly. Unsubmitted anomalies will be counted for leave deduction.

Section D: Admin

System Administration & Data Management

Admin Dashboard & Upload

Monthly Upload Process

  1. Login as admin
  2. Select Office (HQ or Regional)
  3. Upload biometric .xls/.xlsx file(s)
  4. Set parameters: Late time, Early time, Min hours, Allowed anomalies
  5. Click "Upload & Analyze"
  6. System processes and creates anomaly records
Upload multiple months at once. Files are merged by Employee Code.

After Upload

  • View Report — printable attendance report
  • Review & Finalize — review Head decisions, apply leave deductions
  • Excel — download report as .xlsx
  • PDF — print-friendly PDF view

Finalization

  • Review Head's accept/decline decisions
  • Select Exclude (no deduction) or Count (deduct leave)
  • Click "Finalize Decisions"
  • Leave deductions are calculated: 0.5 EL per extra anomaly

Leave Deduction Rules

How Anomalies Are Counted

Anomaly TypeWhen Flagged
Late ArrivalPunch-in after 10:00 AM
Early DeparturePunch-out before 5:00 PM
Short HoursLess than 8 hours worked
Missing DepartureNo out-punch recorded
Missing ArrivalNo in-punch recorded
Note: Multiple anomalies on same day = counted as 1 anomaly date.

Deduction Calculation

Anomaly DaysDeduction
1-2 days0 (free allowance)
3 days0.5 EL
4 days1.0 EL
5 days1.5 EL
N days(N - 2) × 0.5 EL
Accepted justifications are excluded from this count. Only declined or unsubmitted anomalies count towards deduction.
