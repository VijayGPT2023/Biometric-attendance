NPC Biometric Attendance portal scaling plan:
- Currently: HQ only with 1 biometric system, 12 departments, ~147 employees
- Next month: Adding 12 regional offices, each with its own biometric system
- Each regional office produces its own XLS file (may have different format)
- Scale target: 25 departments, 500 employees within 1 month

**Why:** NPC has headquarters + regional offices across India. Each office has independent biometric attendance hardware.

**How to apply:** The app needs an "Office" layer above departments. Admin uploads XLS per office. Employees/Heads are scoped to their office. DG/DDG see all offices.
